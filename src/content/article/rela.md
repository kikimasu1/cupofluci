Put what happened in the past aside, focus on "now". The future also doesn't exist. 

As you can interpreate what happened in the past in different ways, pick the ones that can motivate you do to what you want to do.  

Treat others sincerely, as if they're **you**. 

Don't try to one-up others. Everybody is human and everybody is equal. 

Be authentic, be genuine, be kind, always.

Keep your expectations low.

Put yourself out there.
