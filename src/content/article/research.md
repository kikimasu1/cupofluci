When I went out with a guy from Hinge for the first time, he asked where he should take me.

I was like: dude you're supposed to do this type of research.

Then I took him to a Thai restaurant, a beach with an amazing view, gave him one lollipop from my car because I always carry some with me to prevent low blood sugar. He asked how I found these places, I said: use google maps wisely.

He, as a local Floridian, didn't know much about the surroundings like I did.

I kinda felt bad for him, never saw him again.

Anyways, it's very easy for me to get bored if I got a place figured out. Guess that's why I love to travel so much.

People should spend more time doing research! About where to live, who to be with, what job to pick in the future.

Do your own research as no one else is 100% the same as you, you gotta figure out what you want out of this life.

People should know themselves very well before **committing** to something big.
