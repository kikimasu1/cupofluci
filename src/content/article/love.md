Long story short, find the things you love doing, find the one (or many) you love to be with.

Love is the key to a happy life ;3



Everyday life can be mundane, but once you follow your heart, do what you love, you'll feel a huge difference (or not)

It's worth a try! Love isn't some kind of magical potion to suddenly lift you up, it's the little things that make you feel like life is worth living.

Love makes people non-judgmental of themselves & others' lives, love is inclusive.

Maybe love is the answer to the meaning of life, we were put on this earth to experience love, we're creatures made out of love, we're only here for a short period of time, love is all we can give.

Forget about money & fame & calculating & playing mind games with others, you receive what you give out.



> If you feel that you are in competition with anyone, for anything, you are a loser. - **Nassim Nicholas Taleb**



Stay true, stay genuine, be authentic, the world is your oyster.
