## I'M AMBITIOUSLY FOCUSED ON CREATING

To me, writing is a way of expressing myself, aka creating. I started writing diaries since primary school. A good way to let my creativity out.

I chose tech-focused major for my bachelor's & master's degree because I feel like machine language is more universal, an app update can affect millions of people easily. By combining my love for literature with my computer science knowledge, I can reach a broader audience, essentially getting more feedback, which helps me better understand the world.

In this way, I can make people's lives easier. Self expression is a *form of art* in my opinion, I wouldn't stop expressing, creating, and loving as long as I can.
