---
title: No need for jealousy
description: "no need for such thing at all "
date: 2026-07-09T06:00:00.000Z
---

tldr: just live your best life every single day, then you wouldn't have that energy to be jealous of others lives.



- **Know yourself.** What do you want & don't want? When someone's show-off made you feel something, ask yourself deeply what it is. Different people have different pursuits in life, go for what **YOU** want
- One can only see a **small portion** of other's life. Will you be willing to trade your whole life with theirs?
- **One can't always be a winner.** The grass is always greener on the other side, instead of putting your precious time & energy on jealousing other's achievements, you might as well putting effors in building the life you want
- Ditching the idea of buying expensive stuff will make you "worth more", you didn't bring anything with you when you're born, and you can't take anything with you when you die. If there's an emergency, would you really be thinking about who bought which expensive stuff? Too boring.
- **Live everyday according to your will.** Naturally, you wouldn't care about others. Cuz no matter what their life is, you're the best in your definition. 
- **Sincerely wish others well.** Allowing others to succeed in life will make you assume they're happy for your success as well, thus everybody can be a friend, and life is more enjoyable in this way.

I hope everyone can live best of the best kinda life 🐬
