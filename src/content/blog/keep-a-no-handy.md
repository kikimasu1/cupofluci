title: "Prepare your “no” and keep it handy"
description: "A short note on protecting your time."
date: 2026-07-09T00:00:00.000Z
---

Useful defaults save energy.

When someone asks for your time, it helps to already know what a clear “no” sounds like — kind, short, and final enough that you don’t renegotiate with yourself afterward.

Say less. Mean it.
